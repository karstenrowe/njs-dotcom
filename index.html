Welcome to NJS Equine Therapy

A specialist in Equine Sports Massage and Deep Oscillation® Therapy.

Horses can experience pain and discomfort from muscular problems just as humans can. Some work physically hard on a daily basis, and this can lead to tension and spasm within the muscle. Others that may be less active can also experience these problems due to conformation, injury or other conditions, or equipment.

A number of behavioural or performance related changes can indicate that your horse is experiencing muscular discomfort. A horse may appear that they are being lazy or uncooperative, but this may be your animal’s way of trying to tell you that they are sore.

Sports Massage Therapy aims to relieve this tension and discomfort, improving your horse’s ability to perform. I have seen first hand the positive changes in a variety of different horses, all now feeling much happier in their work.

Deep Oscillation treatment is also offered. This gentle therapy creates an electrostatic field within the tissue, improving circulation to the area, thus improving healing time of certain conditions. It also has pain reducing effects. A number of conditions can benefit from this therapy, for example wounds, tendon and ligament injury, arthritic conditions and muscle tension/atrophy.
